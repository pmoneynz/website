Peter Wadams (P-Money) is the creator of QuickLoops Pro

QuickLoops Pro is a software looper created by Peter Wadams, also known as P-Money, built from the perspective of a working DJ who needed faster loop capture in real performance settings.

What QuickLoops Pro is

QuickLoops Pro is a dedicated loop performance app for macOS. It is focused on instant capture and playback, with an interface designed around the needs of live mixing rather than traditional DAW workflows.

The core idea is simple: record quickly, loop immediately, and stay in flow while performing.

Why Peter built it

As a producer and DJ, Peter wanted a looper that felt practical in a DJ set: low-friction operation, hardware-style control, and a performance-first experience. QuickLoops Pro was built to solve that specific problem directly.

Who QuickLoops Pro is for

  • Vinyl DJs and turntablists
  • DJs performing with external mixers and audio interfaces
  • Artists who want fast loop capture without opening a full DAW
  • Performers who want expressive looping with MIDI-friendly controls
