Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how QuickLoops Pro (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the QuickLoops Pro website and related download/email services at quickloopspro.pmoneymusic.com.

Last updated: January 21, 2026

What we collect

  • Email address (optional): If you enter your email to receive a download link or updates, we collect the email address you submit.
  • Website analytics data: We use Google Analytics 4 and Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how the site is used (for example: page views, clicks, device/browser info, approximate location, and referrer information). These services may use cookies or similar technologies.
  • Download metrics & logs: When you download a .dmg from our site, our infrastructure records basic metadata such as the file requested, timestamp, referrer, and user agent, and may increment download counters.
  • Local storage: The website may store small pieces of data in your browser (for example, to remember whether you’re a returning visitor or whether you’ve already submitted the email form).

How we use information

  • Provide downloads and updates: If you submit your email, we use it to send you a download link and (if applicable) product updates.
  • Operate and improve the site: We use analytics and aggregated metrics to understand performance, fix issues, and improve pages.
  • Security and abuse prevention: Basic request metadata may be used to protect the site and services.

Sharing and third parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us run the site and deliver emails/downloads (for example, analytics providers and hosting infrastructure), and only as needed to provide and maintain the service.

If you download QuickLoops Pro through Apple’s App Store (when applicable), Apple may collect information as part of providing their services. See Apple’s privacy information for details.

Data retention

We keep email addresses for as long as needed to provide download links/updates, or until you ask us to delete your information. Analytics and download metrics may be retained for operational and reporting purposes.

Your choices

  • Email: You can request access, correction, or deletion of your email address by contacting us.
  • Cookies/analytics: You can limit cookies and local storage via your browser settings. Some analytics features may stop working properly if you block all storage.

Children’s privacy

QuickLoops Pro is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we’ll update the “Last updated” date on this page.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about privacy, contact us at support@pmoneymusic.com.
