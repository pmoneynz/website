The Breakthrough: Instant Capture

Most software loopers require menus, tracks, timelines, and configuration. QuickLoops Pro does none of that.

1

Press = Record

Hit a button. Audio from your mixer or turntable starts recording instantly.

2

Press = Loop

Press again. The loop plays back immediately — no delay, no processing.

3

Press = Perform

Press again to cue the loop from the top. Stay in the groove. Get creative.

This is what DJs need — real-time responsiveness, not a DAW workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it really work well with vinyl?

Yes — it's built for capturing vinyl loops (or anything else you like). No other DJ software is required to run QuickLoops Pro.

Does it quantize?

No. This app respects your timing, not a grid. You control when the loop starts and ends.

Do I need a DAW?

No. This replaces a DAW-style looper with something faster and simpler. It's a standalone app.

Does it work with DJ mixers?

Yes — any Core Audio interface works: Rane, Pioneer, Allen & Heath, and more.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 13.5 or later, Apple Silicon or Intel Mac.
