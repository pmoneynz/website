FAQ

Is it really free?

Yes. QuickLoops is freeware. If you love it, consider a tip or sharing with a friend.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 14 Sonoma or later. Works on Apple Silicon and Intel. CoreAudio device recommended.

Does it support MIDI?

Yes — map transport and key actions to your controller. Plug and play.

How do I uninstall?

Quit the app and delete it from Applications. Preferences live in ~/Library/Preferences.
